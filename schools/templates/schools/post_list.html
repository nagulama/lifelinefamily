Welcome to Bukedea Lifeline Schools

Greetings in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ! Bukedea Lifeline schools, located on Okunguro Road kide North Cell Bukedea Town council, Bukedea District, was founded in 2004 by......... making it the oldest private school in the district. It is a co-educational private school with a current ratio of 1:2 girls to boys. It offers general academic subjects, 15 in number. The school, admits students of all faiths. It provides secular education which is in line with the Uganda national goals and objectives of education. The school recognizes the changing global trends and has always organised and re-organised its curriculum to suit the needs of the changing world, aligning it with the national curriculum.
